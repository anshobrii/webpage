Listed below are some of the food items found in Bihar

Litti Chokha

Litti Chokha is the most popular dish in Bihar. Litti is a dough ball made with flour, sattu (roasted gram flour), and spices. It is traditionally roasted over charcoal and served with chokha, a mix of mashed potatoes, brinjal, and tomatoes.


Sattu Paratha

Sattu Paratha is a delicious and healthy dish made with sattu flour, vegetables, and spices. Sattu is a roasted gram flour that is high in protein and fiber and is a staple food in Bihar.


Khaja

Khaja is a sweet dish made of layered dough, ghee, and sugar syrup. It is a popular dessert in Bihar and is often served during festivals and special occasions.


Malpua

Malpua is a sweet pancake-like dessert made with flour, milk, and sugar syrup. It is often flavored with cardamom or saffron and is a popular sweet dish in Bihar.


Makhana Kheer

Makhana Kheer is a sweet dish made with fox nuts, milk, and sugar. It is a popular dessert in Bihar and is often served during festivals and special occasions.


Thekua

Thekua is a sweet biscuit-like snack made with wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee. It is a popular snack during festivals and special occasions in Bihar.



Tilkut

Tilkut is a sweet snack made with sesame seeds, jaggery, and peanuts. It is often served during festivals like Makar Sankranti and is a popular sweet dish in Bihar.


Baigan Bharta

Baigan Bharta is a popular vegetarian dish made with roasted eggplant, onion, tomato, and a variety of spices. It is often served with roti or rice.


Chana Ghugni

Chana Ghugni is a spicy and tangy curry made with chickpeas and a variety of spices. It is often served with flattened rice and is a popular street food in Bihar.


Pittha

Pittha is a steamed dumpling made with rice flour and stuffed with spiced lentils or sweetened coconut. It is a popular snack in Bihar and is often served with chutney.


Makai ki Roti with Sarson ka Saag

Makai ki Roti with Sarson ka Saag is a popular winter dish in Bihar made with flatbread made from cornmeal and a curry made with mustard greens and a variety of spices. It is a nutritious and delicious meal enjoyed by many in Bihar.
