BIHAR
B

Bihar is a state in eastern India. It is the thirteenth-largest state by population and twelfth-largest by area.

I

The word Bihar is derived from the Sanskrit and Pali word "vihara", meaning "abode". Bihar has been home to several ancient civilizations such as Magadha, Maurya, Gupta, Pala, and Mughal Empire.

H

The state of Bihar is the birthplace of two major religions, namely Buddhism and Jainism. It was also the center of the Indian independence movement, with leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, Dr. Rajendra Prasad, and Jayaprakash Narayan hailing from the state.

A

Bihar is known for its rich culture, literature, and music. The famous classical singer Bharat Ratna Pandit Bhimsen Joshi hailed from Bihar. The state is also home to the famous Maithili and Bhojpuri languages.
