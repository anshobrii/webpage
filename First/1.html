Bihar

Bihar is a state in eastern India, bordering Nepal. It is divided into 38 districts and its capital city is Patna.

Ishan

The letter "I" stands for Ishan, which is the direction North-East in Hindu mythology. It also represents the Indian Institute of Technology Patna, which is a prestigious educational institution in Bihar.

History

The letter "H" represents the rich history of Bihar, which dates back to the ancient times of Magadha and the Maurya Empire. Bihar is also known for its contributions to the Indian independence movement, with leaders like Dr. Rajendra Prasad and Jayaprakash Narayan hailing from the state.

Art and Culture

The letter "A" represents the vibrant art and culture of Bihar. The state is known for its Madhubani paintings, Chhath Puja, and folk music and dance forms like Bidesia and Jat-Jatin. Bihar is also home to ancient Buddhist and Jain temples, which attract tourists from all over the world.

Rivers

The letter "R" stands for the rivers that flow through Bihar, including the Ganges, Kosi, and Gandak. These rivers have played a crucial role in the agriculture and economy of Bihar, and also hold religious significance for the people of the state.
