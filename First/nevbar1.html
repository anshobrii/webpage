About Bihar

Bihar is a state in eastern India. It is the third-largest state by population and twelfth-largest by territory. The Ganges River flows through the state, providing water for agriculture and hydroelectric power. Bihar is known for its rich culture, literature, and music, and is home to famous personalities like Bharat Ratna Pandit Bhimsen Joshi. The state is also known for its delicious food and historical landmarks like the ancient Nalanda University.

Culture of Bihar

Bihar has a rich cultural heritage that dates back to ancient times. The state is known for its traditional music and dance forms like Bhojpuri and Maithili, as well as its unique art and handicrafts. Bihar is also home to many religious sites, including the famous Mahabodhi Temple in Bodh Gaya, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Tourism in Bihar

Bihar has a lot to offer in terms of tourism. From historical landmarks like the ancient Nalanda University and the ruins of Vikramshila University to religious sites like the Mahabodhi Temple and the Bodhi Tree, there is something for everyone. The state is also known for its beautiful natural scenery, including the Rajgir Hills and the Valmiki National Park.

Food in Bihar

Bihari cuisine is known for its simplicity and delicious taste. The state is famous for dishes like litti chokha, sattu paratha, and chana ghugni. Bihari cuisine also includes a variety of sweets and desserts like tilkut, balushahi, and lai. Food in Bihar is usually vegetarian, but there are also non-vegetarian options available.
